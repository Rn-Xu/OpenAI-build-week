# Project media plan

Devpost accepts up to 15 images. Use these five in order. Target **1500 × 1000 px**, 3:2, JPG or PNG, and under 5 MB each.

## 1. Project cover

Filename: `01-get-up-cover.png`

Purpose: Communicate the product in one glance.

On-image copy:

- Get up!
- Turn intention into action.
- One small, achievable step at a time.

## 2. Goal input

Filename: `02-goal-input-concept.png`

Purpose: Show how simple it is to begin.

Example input:

> I need to study for tomorrow's exam, but I feel overwhelmed.

Caption: **Tell Get up! what you need to accomplish.**

## 3. First action

Filename: `03-first-action-concept.png`

Purpose: Show the central product idea.

Example response:

> Put your phone aside and open your notes.

Supporting copy:

- Estimated time: 1 minute
- Done
- Make it smaller

Caption: **Start with one small, achievable action.**

## 4. Adaptive guidance

Filename: `04-adaptive-guidance-concept.png`

Purpose: Show that the experience reacts to progress or difficulty.

Interface states:

- Done
- I'm stuck
- Make it smaller
- Next action

Caption: **Adaptive guidance that meets users where they are.**

## 5. Completion

Filename: `05-completion-concept.png`

Purpose: End the gallery with emotional payoff.

On-image copy:

> You did not need to finish everything at once. You only needed to begin.

Caption: **Build momentum through visible progress.**

## Usage warning

Images 2–5 are concept mockups until they are replaced by real application screenshots. Do not present a mockup as proof of implemented behavior. The safest final gallery is one branded cover followed by four screenshots captured from the working Demo.
